One-Time Setup
To sync your library across every device, you need a free JSONBin account. It takes 2 minutes and stores your books in the cloud.
Step 1 — Go to jsonbin.io and click Sign Up Free. Use any email, no card needed.
Step 2 — After signing in, click your profile icon (top right) → API Keys. Copy the Master Key (starts with $2b$...).
Step 3 — Paste it below and click Save. This is stored only on your device to talk to your private bin.
